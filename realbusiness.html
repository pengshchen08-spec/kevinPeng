About CHIYODA Group

Founded in 1969 in Aichi, Japan, CHIYODA Group combines five decades of legacy with contemporary global manufacturing, serving international OEMs and technical partners with reliable wire harness systems.

Founded in 1969 in Aichi, Japan, CHIYODA Group has more than 50 years of expertise in precision wire harness manufacturing. Our heritage is built on quality, customer satisfaction, and continuous innovation in global production.

Total group sales reach 10 Billion JPY, supporting major global brands through integrated engineering, design validation, and advanced factory management.

  • Global footprint with Japan headquarters and Hong Kong regional office.
  • Advanced production plants in Dongguan and Jiaxing with 15,000 m² capacity.
  • 220+ skilled staff delivering consistent quality and rapid turn-key execution.
  • Mission: Customer Satisfaction First with eco-friendly manufacturing and compliance-focused supply chains.

Products

Our product portfolio spans high-reliability harness systems for automotive, medical, office automation and premium consumer segments with robust design, testing and certification coverage.

Automotive & New Energy Harnesses

Medical & Laboratory Equipment

Office Automation Wiring
