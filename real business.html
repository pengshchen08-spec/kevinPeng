How Chiyoda & Qianyutian Work Together

Chiyoda Electronics Co., Ltd. (千代田電子工業) is the group's international commercial interface. Dongguan Qianyutian Electronics Co., Ltd. (东莞千与田电子有限公司) is the core manufacturing entity that executes volume production for export programs.

Chiyoda — Business & Technical Interface
Overseas customers engage Chiyoda for RFQs, drawing review, sampling coordination and account management. Chiyoda consolidates requirements and aligns them with Japan HQ engineering standards before release to production.

Qianyutian — Manufacturing Execution
Once samples and orders are confirmed, production is transferred to Qianyutian's Dongguan plant — equipped with fully automatic processing for crimping, welding, adhesive application and cable assembly at scale.

From Inquiry to Mass Production

A streamlined three-step workflow — Chiyoda handles the commercial front end, Qianyutian delivers the factory output.

Step 01

Chiyoda — Commercial Intake

Submit your specifications or engineering drawings through this portal. The Chiyoda business team provides one-on-one support, technical assessment and sampling preparation for overseas buyers.

Step 02

Qianyutian — Factory Production

After sample approval, orders are seamlessly transferred to Qianyutian's Dongguan plant. Japanese 5S management practices drive efficient, safe and consistent mass production on fully automatic lines.

Step 03

Certified Global Delivery

Products meet ISO 9001, ISO 14001 and UL requirements. Stable, on-time export delivery to international customers through our Hong Kong logistics coordination.
